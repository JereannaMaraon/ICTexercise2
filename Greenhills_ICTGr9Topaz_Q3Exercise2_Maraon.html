Ruud Casper Ruud is a professional tennis player who competes on the ATP Tour. He was born on December 22, 1998. He is the first Norwegian man to reach a Grand Slam final after finishing runner-up at Roland-Garros in 2022. He reached his second Grand Slam final at the US Open in 2022. Having qualified for the event for the second year in a row, Ruud reached the final for the first time, beating Andrey Rublev in the semis, having picked up wins over Taylor Fritz and Felix Auger-Aliassime in the group stages.
